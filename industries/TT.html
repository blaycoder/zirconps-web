Technology & Telecommunication (TT)

Policies are being developed toward tighter control of the ICT sector generally as the Nigerian economy expands along with the country's rising globalization. Hence, the tax authorities start to pay more attention to the issue of expanding infrastructure as well as to cross-border costs associated with software licensing. Similar to this, the Nigerian telecommunications sector can be defined as continually changing due to frequent trend shifts, a challenging business climate, and significant regulatory uncertainty. In order to successfully negotiate a challenging regulatory landscape, the joint industry is expected to continuously reinvent on a basis of enhanced corporate structures and processes.
